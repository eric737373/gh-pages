Labor module help manual

Supplier Portal: https://supplier-portal.yanwenexpress.com/common/login

When the labor supplier enters the supplier portal, log in to the labor account and use the [Labor Management] module

The module contains information that the current labor supplier needs to fill in, as well as some bill details.

Fill in working hours

The [working hours report] module is used by the Yanwen system to dispatch the labor requirements sent to the current labor account.

Each row represents a different batch of labor demand. Each batch can be filled with multiple labor personnel. The filling status is initially unfilled. Click to fill in. The interface is as follows:

  1. In the upper left corner, you can fill in numbers to represent the number of additional labor personnel.

  2. Each line needs to fill in the commuting time, and the working hours will be automatically calculated.

  3. When the form is completed, click Save and the bill will become filled in.

Apply for payment

By default, this page displays the approved [Working Hours Report] items under the current account.

You can see the calculated payment details by clicking [View Bill]
