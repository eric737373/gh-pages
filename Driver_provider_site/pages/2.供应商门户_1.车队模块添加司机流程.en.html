Supplier Portal Add Driver Process

1. ViewSonic driver registration

The icon of our app is and can be searched on Google Store or Apple Store\ [yanwen]，

After the download is completed, click the [Register] button and register according to the following page

After successful registration, you can use your mobile phone number as your ID

  1. Add driver to supplier portal

Website address: https://supplier-portal.yanwenexpress.com/common/login

Site manual: https://yw-helper.yanwenexpress.com/index.html?lang=zh#route-plan

Select [Driver Management], [Driver Profile]

Select [Add Driver] and enter the ViewSonic driver’s mobile phone number in the pop-up panel.

After clicking OK, a successful addition will be displayed in the lower right corner.

  1. Choose a delivery driver

Select [Binning Management], [Binning Files]

Click [Split] of the corresponding box to see the settings in the box

Click [Select Point] and a page will pop up. Click [Select Driver] to see the selectable drivers in the drop-down box.

For specific fence settings, please refer to the help manual.

https://yw-helper.yanwenexpress.com/index.html?lang=zh#route-split

  1. The delivery driver can check the cargo volume of the account

On the mobile app, click to select an account

You can see the data of the boxes being delivered by the current driver, and the goods are waiting to be picked up and delivered.
