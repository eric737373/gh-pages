ADP self-pickup product process

Self-pickup process:

1. Pre-operation:

  1. The customer submits an application and selects a self-collection time period

  2. Customer service confirms self-pickup, adds the order number to the LMS system and notifies the warehouse manager via DingTalk

  3. The person in charge of the warehouse checks the DingTalk notification (or checks the list of items to be picked up in the LMS order list every day) and handles the listing, and at the same time replies to the customer service department for confirmation.

2. Warehouse operations:

Click [Self-pickup goods on shelves]

The goods listing page is as follows. The order number entered comes from the system.

Clicking [Confirm] will display success or error.

Error 1: Waybill does not exist

Error 2: No self-pickup application

Error 3: The goods have been picked up

3. Self-pickup goods handover:

Scan the goods and pick them up successfully

Pickup error

Error 1: The pickup code does not match (ask the pickup person to check the pickup code)

Error 2: The goods are not ready for pickup (please repeat the listing process)

Error 3: The goods have been picked up (check the photos of previous customers picking up goods)
